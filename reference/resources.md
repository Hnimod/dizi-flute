## Grading System
### CCOM (Central Conservatory of Music of China)
- Grades 1-9 + Performance Certificate
- Grade 1-3 = Beginner, Grade 4-6 = Intermediate, Grade 7-9 = Advanced
- Exam books serve as excellent structured learning material even without taking exams
- CCOM Exam Syllabus: https://www.uscycs.org/exam-syllabus.html

### TENG Academy (Singapore)
- Offers structured grading exams
- Details: https://thetengacademy.com/grading/exam-details

### Grading Pieces Discussion
- Forum thread: https://starvoid.proboards.com/thread/2182/dizi-grade-exam-pieces
