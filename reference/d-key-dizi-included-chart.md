# D-Key Dizi — Included Fingering Chart

Parsed from the printed chart (竹笛指法表) that came with the D-key dizi when purchased.

**System**: 全按作5 (all holes closed = 5 / Sol)

## How to Read

- 6 finger holes numbered 6 (leftmost, left hand) to 1 (rightmost, right hand)
- ● = closed | ○ = open | ◐ = half-closed
