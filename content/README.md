# Dizi Flute Learning Course

A structured self-study course for learning dizi (Chinese bamboo flute) from absolute zero to advanced level. No prior music background required.

---

## Course Philosophy

1. **Consistency > Duration** — 30 min daily beats 3 hours on weekends
2. **Slow practice is fast learning** — Always learn passages slowly first, speed up with metronome
3. **Record yourself weekly** — You can't hear yourself accurately while playing
4. **Long tones are non-negotiable** — Do them every session, forever
5. **Relax** — Tension in shoulders, jaw, fingers, stomach = poor tone and fatigue
6. **Blow across, not into** — Air stream splits across the far edge of the blowing hole

## Daily Practice Template

Adjust proportions based on your current level:

| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 5 min | **Breathing** | Diaphragmatic breathing, 4-count in, long exhale |
| 8 min | **Long tones** | Every note you know, steady tone. Add vibrato from Level 4 |
| 7 min | **Scales & tonguing** | Major, pentatonic, various articulations |
| 5 min | **Technique drill** | Current focus: ornaments, double tongue, etc. |
| 10 min | **Repertoire** | Current piece(s) — slow practice first, then tempo |
| **35 min** | **Total** | |

---

## Progress Milestones

- **Week 4** — Produce tone on demand, play notes 1-5
- **Week 8** — Play 2 simple songs from memory, read basic jianpu
- **Month 4** — Play 5+ songs with both octaves, beginning ornaments
- **Month 7** — Playing sounds authentically Chinese with vibrato and ornaments, 8-10 pieces
- **Month 12** — 15+ pieces, CCOM Grade 3-5 comfortable, solid fundamentals
- **Year 2** — 20+ pieces, CCOM Grade 5-7, multiple dizi keys, developing personal style
- **Year 3+** — Concert-level playing, CCOM Grade 7-9, artistic voice
